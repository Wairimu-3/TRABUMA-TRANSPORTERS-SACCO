About Us

Trabuma transporters sacco mission was started with an aim of self regulation within Kenya road networks in corroboration with kenha . Further to this our members savings and credits enjoy massive savings and give credit to members s way of helping members meet their obligation as need arise

Our Vission

Our vision is to establish a self-regulated and efficient transportation network within Kenya's road systems, in collaboration with the Kenya National Highways Authority (KENHA). We strive to provide reliable and safe transportation services while promoting the economic well-being of our members. By fostering a strong savings and credit system, we aim to empower our members to meet their financial obligations and seize opportunities as they arise. As a registered member of the Truckers Association of Kenya, we adhere to the principles of the cooperative movement and operate under the direct supervision of the sub-county cooperative office.
